About

I haven't always been a photographer but it's always been a passion.

  • 2009-2011

    Humble Beginnings

    In high school, I took Intro. to Photography on a whim, hoping to find a form of art I could excel in. I had always been interested in pictures, but wanted to further my interest and learn more about the art form in general. My high school photography classes really inspired me to try and learn the techniques that were necessary to capture and develop my own photos. Since then, I have worked tirelessly to learn as much as possible, and I hope to continue to learn more.

  • 2011-2013

    Event Photography

    In college, I began taking pictures of events for my sorority, extracurricular clubs and other university affairs. Though this was just a hobby, I was quickly asked to bring my camera to more and more activities, which I took as a great compliment! In the later parts of college I began taking senior photos for a few of my friends and garnered the attention of more and more people as they saw the quality of my work.

  • 2013-2014

    Expansion of Skills

    During an additional year of undergraduate studies at Oregon State, I started supplementing my time with photography of close friends proposing, getting engaged and needing professional portraits. As I began branching out, my love for photography continued to grow.

  • 2014-Present

    The Hobby Evolves

    I wrapped up my formal schooling at Portland State University with a Master of Public Health degree in June of 2014, and made a point to focus on photography in conjunction with my studies. I have since expanded my photo avenues, furthered my editing software skills, and gathered enough courage to extend my services to clients other than family and friends.
